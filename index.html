Game Design, Development & Game-Ready Assets

If there's one place where all my passions intersect, it's game dev. Whether it's pixel art, music and sound effects, rigged and animated 3D models, UI design or the programming to put it all together, I'm your guy!

Check out the minigame in the window made in PixiJS, and see if you can beat my high score of 74!

My weapon of choice is the fantastic Godot Engine but I'm adept in C#, C++, JavaScript and many more languages, and highly competent with Unity, Unreal and JavaScript libraries such as PixiJS.
